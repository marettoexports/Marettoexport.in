Who We Are

Maretto Export Import Company

Maretto Export Import Company is an established trading and supply firm headquartered in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh. We specialize in the international trade of fresh agro products, industrial raw materials, and plastic granules — bridging India with global markets.

We connect suppliers and buyers across the globe, offering reliable sourcing, quality assurance, and seamless logistics for high-demand secondary raw materials including paper scraps, plastic polymers, metal scraps, and electronic waste.

Our team is committed to sustainable trade practices, helping industries access quality recycled inputs while reducing environmental impact.
