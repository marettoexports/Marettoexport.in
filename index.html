📦 Export Packing & Cold Chain — Step by Step

Every Step Visible — Zero Compromise on Quality

Our chicken export packing follows a strict 8-step process — from Halal slaughter to reefer container dispatch. Dubai buyers can watch every step live via our Eyes & Ears service.

STEP 01
☪️

Halal Slaughter

Certified Halal slaughtermen. Islamic requirements fully met. GCC & UAE approved process.

STEP 02
🔬

Veterinary Inspection

Every bird inspected by certified government vet. Ante & post-mortem checks. Unfit birds rejected.

STEP 03
🧊

Rapid Chilling

Chilled to 0–4°C within 1 hour of slaughter. Evisceration, washing and cleaning in hygienic facility.

0–4°C
STEP 04
⚖️

Grading & Cutting

Weight-based grading. Whole bird or cut parts per buyer specification. Custom cuts available.

STEP 05
🫙

Vacuum / MAP Packing

Food-grade vacuum packs or Modified Atmosphere Packaging. Extended shelf life. Zero contamination.

STEP 06
📦

Master Carton Packing

HDPE inner bags + corrugated master cartons. Weight, lot number & Halal mark printed. Buyer logo available.

STEP 07
🚛

Cold Storage & Dispatch

Frozen stored at -18°C. Reefer trucks to ICD Patparganj / Mundra port. Temperature logs maintained.

-18°C
STEP 08
📋

Halal Cert · Health Cert · Veterinary Cert · COA · MSDS · Bill of Lading · APEDA docs — all included.
