About Us

India's Rising Global
Trading Firm

Maretto Export Import Company is a Meerut-based, globally connected trading firm specializing in the import and supply of premium polymer materials to the Indian plastics industry.

We source Virgin Plastic Granules and High-Performance Masterbatches directly from the world's best manufacturers and refineries — eliminating middlemen and ensuring quality from origin to factory floor.

Our specialization lies in matching technical data (MFI, Density, Tensile Strength) with the precise requirements of our clients' machines, guaranteeing zero rejection and maximum efficiency.

🏭 Direct Import Model

No middlemen. Products sourced directly from global refineries with full traceability and compliance documentation.

🔬 Technical Matching

MFI values, carrier compatibility and polymer grades matched precisely to your machine and process requirements.

📋 DGFT & PSIC Compliant

All shipments are fully compliant with DGFT regulations. IEC Code: DPJPR4127C. GST: 09DPJPR4127C1ZT.

🚚 CIF Delivery

Seamless delivery to ICD Patparganj and Mundra Port with real-time logistics tracking.

Technical Excellence

We Sell Solutions,
Not Just Products

Our technical approach ensures every product performs perfectly on your specific machinery and process line.

📊

MFI Precision Matching

We verify that the MFI of granules and masterbatch are fully compatible — e.g., PP at 25–35 g/10min matched for injection molding or raffia lines.

🔗

Carrier Compatibility

Masterbatch base polymer is always matched to the client's main polymer — ABS clients receive ABS-based masterbatch, PP clients receive PP-based concentrate.

📄

Lab Reports & COA

Every order is accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis (COA) and Material Safety Data Sheet (MSDS) from the manufacturer's lab.

🧬

Density & Tensile Data

Full material property data sheets including density, tensile strength, elongation at break and heat deflection temperature provided on request.

🛡️

Food-Grade Certification

Select grades of color masterbatches and virgin polymers available with food-contact safety certifications (REACH, RoHS, FDA compliance).

📦

Logistics & Documentation

CIF delivery to ICD Patparganj and Mundra. PSIC and DGFT compliant documentation ensuring hassle-free customs clearance every time.
